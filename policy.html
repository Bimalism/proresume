Privacy Policy

Your privacy is our priority. Learn how ProResume protects your personal data and respects your rights.

Last Updated: December 1, 2023

1. Introduction

Welcome to ProResume ("we," "our," or "us"). We are committed to protecting your personal information and your right to privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our resume builder application.

Important Note

ProResume is designed with privacy as a core principle. Your resume data is processed locally in your browser and never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to export it.

By using ProResume, you consent to the data practices described in this policy. If you do not agree with the terms of this Privacy Policy, please do not access the application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

We collect information that you voluntarily provide when using ProResume:

  • Resume Content: Personal details, work experience, education, skills, and other information you enter into your resume
  • Contact Information: Name, email address, phone number, and location when provided
  • Account Information: If you create an account, we collect username and password
  • Communication Data: Information you provide when contacting our support team

2.2 Automatically Collected Information

When you use ProResume, we automatically collect:

  • Device Information: Browser type, operating system, device type
  • Usage Data: Pages visited, features used, time spent on the application
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser version, screen resolution
  • Cookies Data: Session cookies for functionality (see Cookie Policy below)

2.3 Local Storage

ProResume uses your browser's local storage to:

  • Save your resume progress automatically
  • Store your template preferences
  • Remember your form data between sessions
  • Save your settings and customizations
Key Privacy Feature

Your resume data is stored locally in your browser using localStorage and sessionStorage. This means your personal information never leaves your device unless you explicitly export your resume as a PDF.

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Data Storage and Security

4.1 Where Your Data is Stored

All resume data is stored locally in your browser using:

  • localStorage: Persistent storage that remains until you clear browser data
  • sessionStorage: Temporary storage that clears when you close the browser
  • IndexedDB: For larger resume files and attachments (if applicable)

4.2 Security Measures

We implement appropriate technical and organizational security measures:

  • HTTPS encryption for all data transmission
  • Same-origin policy enforcement
  • Regular security assessments
  • Secure coding practices
  • Access controls and authentication

4.3 Data Retention

We retain your information only for as long as necessary:

  • Resume Data: Stored locally in your browser until you clear it
  • Account Data: Until you delete your account (if applicable)
  • Support Communications: 2 years from last contact
  • Analytics Data: 26 months from collection

5. Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

Your Rights Under GDPR, CCPA, and Other Regulations

Even though ProResume stores data locally, we respect global privacy standards and provide you with control over your information.

5.1 Your Rights Include:

  • Right to Access: Request a copy of your personal data
  • Right to Rectification: Correct inaccurate or incomplete data
  • Right to Erasure: Request deletion of your data (right to be forgotten)
  • Right to Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data
  • Right to Data Portability: Receive your data in a structured format
  • Right to Object: Object to certain types of processing
  • Right to Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent at any time

5.2 How to Exercise Your Rights

To exercise any of these rights, you can:

  1. Clear your browser data to remove locally stored resume information
  2. Use the "Reset Form" feature in the application
  3. Contact us at privacy@proresume.com
  4. Use the "Delete Account" feature if you have created an account

We will respond to your request within 30 days and will not charge you for exercising your rights.

7. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

ProResume uses minimal cookies and tracking technologies:

7.1 Essential Cookies

  • Session Cookies: Necessary for application functionality
  • Preference Cookies: Remember your settings and choices
  • Security Cookies: Support security features

7.2 How to Control Cookies

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Options > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and data stored
Important Note

Disabling essential cookies may affect ProResume's functionality, including the ability to save your progress and remember your preferences.

8. Children's Privacy

ProResume is not intended for children under the age of 16. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us immediately.

If we learn that we have collected personal information from a child under 16, we will delete that information as quickly as possible.

9. International Data Transfers

Since ProResume processes data locally in your browser, no international data transfers occur for your resume information.

However, if you contact our support team or use certain third-party services, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside of your country of residence. We ensure appropriate safeguards are in place for such transfers.

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by:

  • Posting the new Privacy Policy on this page
  • Updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
  • Displaying a notification in the application for significant changes
  • Sending an email to registered users (if applicable)

You are advised to review this Privacy Policy periodically for any changes. Changes to this Privacy Policy are effective when they are posted on this page.
