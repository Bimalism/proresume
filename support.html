Frequently Asked Questions

How do I download my resume as PDF?

To download your resume as PDF:

  1. Complete your resume in the resume builder
  2. Click the "Download PDF" button in the preview section
  3. Wait for the PDF generation to complete (usually takes 10-30 seconds)
  4. Save the PDF file to your device

If you're having issues, try using a different browser or check your internet connection.

My template isn't loading properly. What should I do?

If your template isn't loading:

  • Refresh the page (Ctrl + F5 for hard refresh)
  • Clear your browser cache
  • Try using a different browser (Chrome works best)
  • Check your internet connection
  • Make sure JavaScript is enabled in your browser

If the problem persists, please submit a complaint using the form above.

Can I save my progress and continue later?

Yes! ProResume automatically saves your progress in your browser's local storage. You can also manually save your progress by clicking the "Save Progress" button in the top right corner.

To continue later, simply return to the website from the same browser and device, and your work will be automatically loaded.

How long does support take to respond?

Our typical response times are:

  • Urgent issues: Within 2 hours
  • High priority: Within 6 hours
  • Normal issues: Within 24 hours
  • Feature requests: Within 48 hours

You'll receive updates via email or WhatsApp based on your preferred contact method.

Is my data safe and private?

Yes, your data is completely private and secure:

  • All data is stored locally in your browser
  • We don't store your resume data on our servers
  • No personal information is shared with third parties
  • PDFs are generated locally in your browser
  • We use HTTPS encryption for all communications
